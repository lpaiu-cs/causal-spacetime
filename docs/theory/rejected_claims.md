# Rejected Claims

The project explicitly rejects these weak or misleading claims:

- "time is information-transfer speed",
- "space is information-transfer speed",
- "finite signal speed alone gives relativity",
- "the simulations prove spacetime is information",
- "causal order alone gives absolute metric scale",
- "1+1D validation proves physical spacetime emergence",
- "dimension is purely information",
- "this replaces relativity".

The conservative claim is narrower: causal/accessibility order, counting
measure, and observer protocols can be studied as ingredients in operational
reconstruction procedures inside controlled models.
