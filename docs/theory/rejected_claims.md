# Rejected Claims

The project explicitly rejects these weak or misleading claims:

- "time is information-transfer speed",
- "space is information-transfer speed",
- "finite signal speed alone gives relativity",
- "the simulations prove spacetime is information",
- "causal order alone gives absolute metric scale",
- "causal order alone gives physical volume or the conformal factor",
- "distance order alone gives full metric geometry",
- "observer-relative distance order is absolute space",
- "ratios are primitive",
- "metric tensor is fundamental in this program",
- "curvature values are primitive",
- "any arbitrary distance order admits a Lorentzian representation",
- "event positions alone determine metric scale",
- "event distribution proves spacetime emergence",
- "causal order alone gives a manifold or a unique observer atlas",
- "causal order alone gives signed spatial coordinates or affine translations",
- "we simulated a black hole",
- "event horizons are proven to be information",
- "inaccessible events do not exist",
- "1+1D validation proves physical spacetime emergence",
- "dimension is purely information",
- "this replaces relativity".

The conservative claim is narrower: causal/accessibility order, counting
measure, and observer protocols can be studied as ingredients in operational
reconstruction procedures inside controlled models.
