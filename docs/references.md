# References

This list is intentionally short for the first milestone and should be expanded
as the simulation scope grows.

- L. Bombelli, J. Lee, D. Meyer, and R. D. Sorkin, "Space-Time as a Causal Set",
  Physical Review Letters, 1987.
- R. D. Sorkin, "Causal Sets: Discrete Gravity", in *Lectures on Quantum
  Gravity*, 2005.
- G. Brightwell and R. Gregory, "Structure of random discrete spacetime",
  Physical Review Letters, 1991.
- D. D. Reid, "Introduction to causal sets: an alternate view of spacetime
  structure", Canadian Journal of Physics, 2001.
